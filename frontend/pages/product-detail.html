Product Description

The new MacBook Pro delivers game-changing performance for pro users. With the powerful M2 chip, you get groundbreaking performance and amazing battery life. It features a stunning Retina display, a Magic Keyboard, and versatile connectivity for everyday use.

Revolutionary Apple silicon
The M2 chip begins the next chapter for Apple silicon, which has transformed the Mac lineup. With incredible performance, custom technologies, and industry-leading power efficiency, M2 is the next generation of Apple silicon for Mac.

Up to 20 hours of battery life
The efficiency of the M2 chip allows MacBook Pro to deliver the same extraordinary battery life as the previous generation: up to 20 hours of video playback.

Active cooling system
The active cooling system sustains incredible performance. Thanks to its advanced thermal system and the efficiency of Apple silicon, MacBook Pro can sustain pro levels of performance, so you can run CPU- and GPU-intensive tasks for hours on end.

Retina display
The 13.3-inch Retina display features 500 nits of brightness and P3 wide color for vibrant images and incredible detail. True Tone technology automatically adjusts the white point of the display to match the color temperature of your environment, making web pages and email look as natural as the printed page.
